\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
A class representing a sphere. 

This class provides methods for initializing, updating, and generating the \mbox{\hyperlink{classnetdem_1_1_sphere}{Sphere}} object. It also implements operations such as computing support points, signed distance, and surface point. The sphere is defined by its radius and center position.
