\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
A class used to control the displacement of walls in a DEM simulation. 

The \mbox{\hyperlink{classnetdem_1_1_wall_disp_control}{Wall\+Disp\+Control}} class is used to control the displacement of walls in a DEM simulation by setting their velocities and spins.
