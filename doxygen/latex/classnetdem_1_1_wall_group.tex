\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
A class used to group walls in a DEM simulation. 

The \mbox{\hyperlink{classnetdem_1_1_wall_group}{Wall\+Group}} class is used to group walls together for various operations, such as setting their velocity or spin properties.
