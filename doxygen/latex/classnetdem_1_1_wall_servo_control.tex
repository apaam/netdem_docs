\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
A class used to control the pressure of walls in a DEM simulation. 

The \mbox{\hyperlink{classnetdem_1_1_wall_servo_control}{Wall\+Servo\+Control}} class is used to control the pressure of walls in a DEM simulation by adjusting their position based on the target pressure and achieved pressure.
