\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
shape interface\+:
\begin{DoxyItemize}
\item volume, inertia\+: volume and inertia in the principal directions.
\item size\+: diameter of the sphere with the same volume
\item bound\+\_\+sphere\+\_\+radius\+: the radius of the smallest sphere that encloses the particle. This variable is used for broad-\/phase contact detection.
\item skin\+: for broad-\/phase contact detection. 
\end{DoxyItemize}
