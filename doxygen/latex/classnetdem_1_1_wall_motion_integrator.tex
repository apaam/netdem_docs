\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
A class used to integrate the motion of walls in a DEM simulation. 

The \mbox{\hyperlink{classnetdem_1_1_wall_motion_integrator}{Wall\+Motion\+Integrator}} class is used to integrate the motion of walls in a DEM simulation by updating their positions and orientations based on their velocities and spins.
