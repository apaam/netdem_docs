\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
A class used to evaluate the energy of particles in a DEM simulation. 

The \mbox{\hyperlink{classnetdem_1_1_particle_energy_evaluator}{Particle\+Energy\+Evaluator}} class calculates the energy of a specified set of particles, and stores this information in a list of \mbox{\hyperlink{structnetdem_1_1_particle_energy}{Particle\+Energy}} structs.
