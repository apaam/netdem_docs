Detailed Description

  • kn, kt: normal and tangential contact stiffness. Generally, in the linear spring contact model, the contact force is the contact stiffness multiplied by the contact deformation.
  • mu: friction coefficient, to incoorporate the Columnb's friction law. According to this law, the tangential force should not exceed the normal force multiplied by the friction coefficient.
