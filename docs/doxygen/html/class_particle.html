Detailed Description

  • vel_m0p5[3], spin_principal_m0p5[3]: translational and rotational velocities at the previous half timestep.
  • rot_angle_cos, rot_angle_sin: cos and sin results of the Euler rotation angle. These two variables are fequently used and defined herein to avoid the repeated calculation.
  • shape: shape of the particle. (Currently, the particle size is incoorporated in the shape. Will plan to seperate size from shape later.)
  • bound_min, bound_max: the lower and upper bounds of a smallest rectangle that encolse the particle.
  • material_type: material type of this particle. This variable is used to find the contact model, as different materials have different contact behaviors.
  • density: particle density
  • mass: particle mass
  • moi_principal: mass moment of inertial of the particle in the principal directions.
  • damp_global: damping coefficient to dissipate energy. The global resistant forces are proportional to the damping coefficient and velcity.
  • pos: particle centroid.
  • rot_angle, rot_axis: Euler's rotation angle and axis
  • vel: translational velocity
  • spin, spin_principal: rotational velocity in the global and local (principal) coordinate system
  • force, moment, moment_principal forces and moments in the global and local (principal) coordinate system
  • contact_pp_map: a map with the key being the particles in contact, and the value being the corresponding contacts.
  • contact_pw_map: a map with the key being the walls in contact, and the value being the corresponding contacts.
