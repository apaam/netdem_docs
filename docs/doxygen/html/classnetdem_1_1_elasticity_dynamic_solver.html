Detailed Description

Adapted from mfem ex10.cpp Nonlinear operator of the form: k --> M*k + E(u + dt*(v + dt*k)) + S*(v + dt*k) - F, where M is the mass matrix, E is material matrix, S is viscosity, F is body and external force, v and u are vel and pos, k is acceralation, and dt is timestep.
