\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
A class used to group particles in a DEM simulation. 

The \mbox{\hyperlink{classnetdem_1_1_particle_group}{Particle\+Group}} class is used to group particles together for various operations, such as setting their velocity or spin properties.
