\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
\mbox{\hyperlink{classnetdem_1_1_contact_solver_factory}{Contact\+Solver\+Factory}} behaviors as a factory to get solvers for contact detection and resolution.
\begin{DoxyItemize}
\item solver\+\_\+pp\+\_\+pool\+: stores the solvers for different pairs of shapes. When contact needs to be checked for two particles, solvers is gotten from the solver\+\_\+pp\+\_\+pool based on the shapes of the two particles.
\item solver\+\_\+pw\+\_\+pool\+: similar to the solver\+\_\+pp\+\_\+pool, whereas it is for particle and wall contacts. 
\end{DoxyItemize}
