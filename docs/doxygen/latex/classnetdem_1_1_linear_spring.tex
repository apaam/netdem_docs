\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}

\begin{DoxyItemize}
\item kn, kt\+: normal and tangential contact stiffness. Generally, in the linear spring contact model, the contact force is the contact stiffness multiplied by the contact deformation.
\item mu\+: friction coefficient, to incoorporate the Columnb\textquotesingle{}s friction law. According to this law, the tangential force should not exceed the normal force multiplied by the friction coefficient. 
\end{DoxyItemize}
